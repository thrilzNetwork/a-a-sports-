# Design System - A&A SPORT

## Brand Identity

"A&A SPORT: Más que una marca, una nueva historia."
"Pasión a tu medida."

A&A SPORT is a sportswear brand with an energetic, athletic identity focused on passion, performance, and self-expression. The brand conveys energy, competition, and personal style.

---

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere

The design captures the intensity of sports culture with bold typography, dynamic color usage, and an athletic aesthetic. Clean lines meet energetic accents — the brand speaks to athletes and fashion-forward individuals who value both performance and style.

**Key Characteristics:**
- Bold athletic typography with uppercase display text
- High-contrast color combinations (dark backgrounds with vibrant accents)
- Dynamic energy with motion-inspired design elements
- Clean, modern sportswear aesthetic
- Minimal card borders with bold visual statements

---

## 2. Color Palette & Roles

### Primary

- **A&A Black** (`#0D0D0D`): Primary — text, headers, button backgrounds, strong contrast base
- **A&A White** (`#FFFFFF`): Primary page canvas, text on dark, clean surfaces

### Brand Accent Colors

- **Passion Red** (`#E63946`): Primary accent — energy, passion, action elements
- **Athletic Blue** (`#1D3557`): Secondary accent — trust, performance, depth
- **Victory Gold** (`#F4A261`): Highlight accent — achievement, premium feel

### Surface & Background

- **Snow** (`#FAFAFA`): Lightest surface, subtle differentiation
- **Light Gray** (`#F5F5F5`): Secondary background, card fills
- **Hover Gray** (`#E8E8E8`): Hover state background
- **Dark Surface** (`#1A1A1A`): Primary background for inverted sections
- **Charcoal** (`#2B2B2B`): Dark sections, footer backgrounds

### Text & Borders

- **Primary Text** (`#0D0D0D`): Main body text, headings
- **Secondary Text** (`#6B6B6B`): Descriptive copy, metadata
- **Muted Text** (`#9B9B9B`): Timestamps, subtle info
- **Border Light** (`#E0E0E0`): Subtle dividers
- **Border Strong** (`#0D0D0D`): Active borders, emphasized dividers

### Semantic

- **Error Red** (`#D32F2F`): Errors, critical alerts
- **Success Green** (`#2E7D32`): Confirmation, positive states
- **Info Blue** (`#1976D2`): Links, informational elements

## 3. Typography Rules

### Font Family

**Display/Athletic:** Impact, Oswald, or similar bold condensed sans-serif
- Bold uppercase for headlines
- Athletic, powerful feel

**Heading:** Montserrat Semibold (600)
- Clean, modern headings

**Body:** Montserrat Regular (400), Open Sans
- Readable body text with modern几何感

### Hierarchy

| Role | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|------|------|--------|-------------|
| Hero Display | 48-64px | 700 | 1.0-1.1 |
| Heading 1 | 36px | 700 | 1.2 |
| Heading 2 | 28px | 600 | 1.25 |
| Heading 3 | 20px | 600 | 1.35 |
| Body Large | 16px | 400 | 1.6 |
| Body | 14px | 400 | 1.6 |
| Caption | 12px | 500 | 1.5 |

### Principles

- Display text: Bold, uppercase for emphasis
- Headings: Semibold for clarity
- Body: Regular weight for readability
- Generous line-height for comfortable reading

## 4. Component Stylings

### Buttons

**Primary (CTA)**
- Background: Passion Red (`#E63946`)
- Text: White (`#FFFFFF`), 14-16px/600
- Border: none
- Border radius: 4-8px (slightly rounded for athletic feel)
- Padding: 12px 24px
- Hover: Darken to `#C62828`
- Active: Scale 0.98
- Transition: all 200ms ease

**Primary Dark**
- Background: A&A Black (`#0D0D0D`)
- Text: White
- Hover: `#333333`

**Secondary**
- Background: transparent
- Text: A&A Black (`#0D0D0D`)
- Border: 2px solid `#0D0D0D`
- Border radius: 4-8px
- Hover: Background `#0D0D0D`, text white

**Small**
- Padding: 8px 16px
- Font size: 12-14px

### Cards & Product Display

- Background: White (`#FFFFFF`)
- Border: none or 1px subtle `#E0E0E0`
- Border radius: 8px
- Shadow: none or subtle `0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)`
- Hover: Subtle lift or border color change
- Product image: Full bleed or slight padding
- Padding: 16px internal

### Navigation

- Background: White or transparent with blur
- Height: 60-72px desktop
- Logo: Bold "A&A SPORT" text or icon
- Links: 14-16px semibold
- Mobile: Hamburger menu, full-screen overlay
- Sticky on scroll with subtle shadow

### Promotional Banners

- Background: A&A Black or Passion Red
- White text
- Centered, 12px padding
- Full width
- Bold uppercase messaging

## 6. Do's and Don'ts

### Do

- Use bold display typography for headlines (uppercase, athletic feel)
- Keep buttons slightly rounded (4-8px radius)
- Use Passion Red as primary CTA color
- Apply dark backgrounds for emphasis sections
- Include clean, full-bleed product images
- Use modern geometric sans-serif fonts

### Don't

- Don't add heavy shadows to cards
- Don't use overly rounded buttons (too soft loses athletic edge)
- Don't mix too many accent colors — stick to the palette
- Don't use serif fonts (breaks athletic aesthetic)
- Don't overcrowd product grids — maintain breathing room
